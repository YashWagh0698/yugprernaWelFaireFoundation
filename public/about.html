Who We Are

About YugPrerna Welfare Foundation

YugPrerna Welfare Foundation is a registered nonprofit organisation committed to designing and delivering structured, replicable social impact programs across India. Founded to address systemic exclusion in employment, education, and economic participation, the Foundation operates through a governance-driven model aligned with national development priorities and corporate social responsibility frameworks.

Social Problems We Address

  • → High youth unemployment and skill mismatches in underserved communities
  • → Limited economic participation among women from low-income backgrounds
  • → Systemic exclusion of persons with disabilities from formal employment
  • → Inadequate access to quality education and vocational capacity building

Our Organisational Model

  • ✓ Governed by a Board of Trustees with defined oversight and reporting protocols
  • ✓ Programs aligned with national policy and international inclusion standards
  • ✓ M&E system tracks beneficiary progress, outcomes, and cost-efficiency
  • ✓ Replication-ready program modules scalable to new geographies

Where We're Going

Vision

A socially inclusive India where all individuals — regardless of gender, ability, or background — have equitable access to employment, education, and economic opportunity.

How We Get There

Mission

  • Skill and certify youth annually for formal employment through structured, industry-aligned training programs.
  • Enable economic independence for women through enterprise development, financial literacy, and livelihood programs.
  • Facilitate employment-ready inclusion for persons with disabilities through assistive technology and employer partnerships.
