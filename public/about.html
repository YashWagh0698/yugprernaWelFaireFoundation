About Us

YugPrerna Welfare Foundation is a registered nonprofit organisation committed to designing and delivering structured, replicable social impact programs across India. Founded to address systemic exclusion in employment, education, and economic participation, the Foundation operates through a governance-driven model aligned with national development priorities and corporate social responsibility frameworks.

Social Problems Addressed

  • High youth unemployment and skill mismatches in underserved communities
  • Limited economic participation among women from low-income backgrounds
  • Systemic exclusion of persons with disabilities from formal employment
  • Inadequate access to quality education and vocational capacity building

Organisational Model

  • Governed by a Board of Trustees with defined oversight, accountability, and reporting protocols
  • Programs designed using an inclusion framework aligned with national policy and international standards
  • Delivery channels include community centers, institutional partners, and digital platforms
  • Monitoring & Evaluation system tracks beneficiary progress, outcomes, and cost-efficiency
  • Replication-ready program modules scalable to new geographies and partner ecosystems
