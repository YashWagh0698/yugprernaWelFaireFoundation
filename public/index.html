Initiatives

Present & Future Initiatives

Our current active work and what's on the horizon — aligned with YugPrerna's founding objectives and national social development priorities.

● Currently Active

SITDP — Internship & Talent Pipeline

Providing structured, audit-safe internship pathways for students and graduates across 10 role categories in NGO administration, technology, research, HR, legal, and field operations.

Objective: Build a sustainable, ethically governed talent pipeline for the social sector.

● Currently Active

Volunteer Ecosystem Program (VEP/YNVEC)

Connecting Expert Professionals, Working Professionals, Students, and Community Members through a four-tier volunteer framework with structured mentorship and recognition systems.

Objective: Create a lifelong, loyal, and impactful volunteer community across India.

● Currently Active

Summer Internship Program 2026

45-day hybrid internship open to students across India in IT, HR, Digital Marketing, Research, NGO Administration, and CEO Office domains. Certificates, LoRs, and LinkedIn-verified outcomes.

Objective: Provide purposeful, impact-driven summer learning for young professionals.

◌ Upcoming

Inclusive Matrimonial Portal

A fully accessible matrimonial platform designed specifically for persons with disabilities — WCAG 2.1 AA compliant, screen-reader friendly, and RPwD Act 2016 aligned.

Objective: Facilitate inclusive social connections for the disability community.

◌ Upcoming

Accessible Cooking Course

A structured vocational training program teaching culinary skills with fully accessible content — tactile guides, audio instructions, and large-print materials for blind and low-vision learners.

Objective: Enable livelihood generation for persons with visual and other disabilities.

◌ Upcoming

Technical Skill Development for PwD

A dedicated digital and technical skills programme for Persons with Disabilities — covering assistive technology, accessible software, and job-ready technical certifications.

Objective: Bridge the digital divide for the disability community in formal employment.
