Who We Are

Built for Transparent, Measurable Social Impact

YugPrerna Welfare Foundation addresses systemic exclusion in employment, education, and economic participation through a governance-driven model aligned with national development priorities and CSR frameworks.

🎓

Skill Development

Industry-aligned training and certification for underserved youth. NSQF-compliant programs with 90-day placement tracking.

👩‍💼

Women Empowerment

Enterprise development, financial literacy, SHG facilitation, and market linkages for women from low-income households.

♿

Disability Inclusion

Accessible training, assistive technology integration, and employer sensitisation for persons with disabilities. RPwD Act 2016 compliant.

📚

Education & Capacity

Remedial learning, teacher training, and digital tools for out-of-school youth and first-generation learners.

"At YugPrerna Welfare Foundation, we hold ourselves to the standards of transparency, measurable delivery, and partner accountability. Every program we run is designed for verifiable outcomes — tracked through a robust M&E system and reported to stakeholders on an annual basis."

— Founder & CEO, YugPrerna Welfare Foundation
