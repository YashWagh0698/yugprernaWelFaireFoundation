About YugPrerna

YugPrerna Welfare Foundation is a registered nonprofit organisation committed to designing and delivering structured, replicable social impact programs across India. Our work addresses systemic exclusion in employment, education, and economic participation through a governance-driven model aligned with national development priorities and CSR frameworks.

Our Key Focus Areas

Skill Development

Industry-aligned training and placement support for underserved youth, with measurable outcomes and tracking.

Women Empowerment

Enterprise development, financial literacy, and market linkages for women from low-income backgrounds.

Disability Inclusion

Accessible training, assistive technology integration, and employer sensitisation for persons with disabilities.

Education & Capacity Building

Remedial learning, teacher training, and digital tools for sustainable community development.

Why Choose Us

  • Governance & Board Oversight with documented accountability
  • Data-driven reporting and annual impact reports
  • Replication-ready programs aligned with CSR and compliance requirements
