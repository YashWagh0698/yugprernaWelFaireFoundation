Our Programs

Programs are designed for measurable impact across four strategic focus areas: skill development, women empowerment, disability inclusion, and education capacity building.

Skill Development & Workforce Readiness

Structured training aligned with NSQF and industry standards, delivered through residential centres, mobile units, and digital platforms.

Women Empowerment & Economic Inclusion

Livelihood programs, financial literacy, business management, and market access support for women from marginalized households.

Disability Inclusion & Employment Enablement

Accessible learning, employer sensitisation, inclusive hiring roadmaps, and partnerships with DPOs and rehabilitation centers.

Education & Capacity Building

Remedial education, teacher training, digital tools, and partnerships with government schools and DIET centers.

Program Impact

Each program is supported by monitoring and evaluation systems, third-party validation, and governance-ready documentation for corporate partners.

  • Structured beneficiary tracking and outcomes measurement
  • Quarterly dashboards and annual reports for partners
  • Compliance with CSR and national policy priorities
