Your Impact

Where Your Donation Goes

Every rupee is accounted for through our Monitoring & Evaluation system. Impact reports are shared with donors annually.

🎓

Skill Development

Funds industry-aligned training, NSQF-compliant programs, and placement support for underserved youth across India.

♿

Disability Inclusion

Provides assistive technology, accessible training materials, and employer sensitisation programs for PwD communities.

👩

Women Empowerment

Supports enterprise development, financial literacy training, and SHG facilitation for women from low-income households.

📚

Education Access

Enables remedial learning, digital tools for first-generation learners, and teacher training in underserved communities.

💼

Internship Programs

Funds structured internships (SITDP & SIP) that give real work experience to students and graduates from all backgrounds.

🌐

Volunteer Ecosystem

Builds and sustains the YNVEC volunteer network — connecting expert professionals with communities who need their knowledge.
