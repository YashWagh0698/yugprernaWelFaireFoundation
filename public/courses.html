Our Programmes

Built for Real Life, Designed for Everyone

At YugPrerna Welfare Foundation, we believe that quality education should never be gatekept by disability, geography, or income. Every programme we offer is built with one purpose: to give learners a practical skill they can use to earn, grow, and stand independently.

Our courses are not just lessons — they are career launchpads. Each programme is designed with screen-reader compatibility (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver), keyboard-only navigation, accessible study materials, and flexible learning modes.

Computer Literacy & Digital Skills for Visually Impaired Learners

Your keyboard is your superpower. We will teach you to use it.

⏱ 3 Months 🌐 Online / Offline / Hybrid 🗣 EN · HI · MR 📖 NVDA & JAWS Ready

A fully keyboard-based, screen-reader-driven computer literacy programme built exclusively for visually impaired learners. No mouse. No prior experience needed. Just you, a keyboard, and the determination to learn.

  • Week 1–4: Computer basics, keyboard mastery, NVDA screen reader
  • Week 5–8: MS Word document creation, file & folder management
  • Week 9–10: MS Excel basics — data entry, simple calculations
  • Week 11–12: PowerPoint, internet safety, Gmail & email usage

Programme Structure

  • Month 1: Keyboard mastery, NVDA commands, Windows navigation, touch typing
  • Month 2: MS Word document creation, formatting, file/folder organisation
  • Month 3: Excel basics, PowerPoint, internet, email, online safety

Who Is This For?

  • Visually impaired students and youth (any age, beginner level)
  • Persons who have never used a computer before
  • VI individuals preparing for employment or higher education

Career Outcomes

  • Apply for data-entry, documentation, or computer-operator roles
  • Access government scheme portals independently
  • Build confidence to enrol in advanced digital skill courses

Our Commitment

♿ Our Accessibility Promise — No Learner Left Behind

At YugPrerna Welfare Foundation, accessibility is not an afterthought — it is how we build everything from the beginning. Every course, every material, and every form has been designed to work for every learner, regardless of disability.

🖥️

Screen Reader Ready

All course materials and web content are fully compatible with NVDA, JAWS, and VoiceOver screen readers.

⌨️

Keyboard-Only Navigation

Every page, form, and interactive element can be operated without a mouse — essential for VI and motor-impaired learners.

📽️

Captioned Videos

All training videos have accurate captions and full transcripts available for download.

🔤

Large Print & Audio

Every study material is available in large print (16pt minimum) and audio format on request.

⏱️

No Timed Tasks

We do not believe in time pressure. Every learner completes assessments at their own pace.

🗣️

Hindi Language Support

Full Hindi (and Marathi) language support available across all programmes for regional learners.
